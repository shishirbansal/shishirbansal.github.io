What this site is about

This website is intentionally simple: a place to collect ideas in public without needing to over-polish everything. Some posts will be long-form technical writing, others will be short field notes from projects, books, conversations, or daily routines.

Topics will include software architecture, engineering productivity, travel reflections, and small experiments in consistency, health, and mindset. If it is useful, honest, or worth remembering, it belongs here.
